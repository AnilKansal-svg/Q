Introduction

Qyverra Technologies is committed to the highest standards of ethical conduct and integrity in all our business operations. While there is no legal requirement in India to publish a modern slavery statement, we recognize the importance of addressing human rights issues, including forced labor, child labor, and modern slavery. As a company operating in international markets, we voluntarily uphold practices aligned with global ethical expectations.

Our Business

Qyverra Technologies is a high-tech IT solutions and services provider headquartered in [City, State, India], offering advanced digital transformation, cloud, AI/ML, and cybersecurity services. We operate in a professional industry and maintain a workforce composed of skilled and qualified personnel.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to:

  • Operating in full compliance with Indian labor laws and international ethical standards
  • Ensuring that modern slavery, human trafficking, or forced labor have no place in our business or supply chain
  • Promoting safe, fair, and inclusive working environments

    • Our Supply Chain

    Qyverra Technologies primarily partners with global software vendors, hardware providers, cloud service platforms, and specialized contractors. While the risk of modern slavery in our supply chain is considered low, we remain vigilant by:

  • Conducting due diligence on key suppliers
  • Preferring partners who demonstrate ethical labor practices
  • Reviewing and updating procurement practices as necessary

    • Employment Practices

    At Qyverra Technologies:

  • Employment is freely chosen, and no forced or involuntary labor is tolerated
  • Child labor is strictly prohibited in accordance with Indian law
  • All employees receive fair compensation and benefits aligned with applicable labor standards
  • We uphold a safe, respectful, and non-discriminatory workplace

    • Awareness and Training

    We promote awareness of ethical conduct through:

  • Internal communications and leadership engagement on labor rights
  • Oversight by our HR and compliance teams on recruitment and third-party staffing
  • Consideration of human rights during onboarding of employees and vendors

    • Future Commitment

    As Qyverra Technologies continues to grow, we are committed to strengthening our ethical sourcing and employment practices. We will regularly review and update our policies to remain aligned with global expectations and best practices.
