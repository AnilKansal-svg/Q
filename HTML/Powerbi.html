What is Power BI?

Power BI is a business analytics solution that lets you visualize your data and share insights across your organization, or embed them in your app or website. Connect to hundreds of data sources and bring your data to life with live dashboards and reports. With AI-powered analytics, automated insights, and seamless integration with Microsoft 365, Power BI helps you make data-driven decisions faster and more confidently.

What is Power BI used for?

Power BI is used for creating interactive reports and dashboards, monitoring key performance indicators, and sharing business insights across organizations. Companies use Power BI to track sales performance, analyze customer behavior, monitor operational metrics, and visualize financial data. With self-service analytics capabilities, business users can easily explore data and create reports without technical expertise.

What are the main features Power BI?

Power BI offers interactive data visualization, real-time dashboards, natural language queries with Q&A, automated insights powered by AI, and seamless integration with Microsoft 365 and hundreds of data sources. Features include drag-and-drop report creation, mobile accessibility, collaborative workspaces, row-level security, and the ability to embed reports in websites and applications.
