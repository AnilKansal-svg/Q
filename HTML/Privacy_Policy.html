Privacy Policy

Qyverra Technologies Limited, together with its subsidiaries (hereinafter referred to as “Qyverra” or “we”), leverages deep domain expertise, enterprise-grade solutions, and advanced digital technologies to transform business processes across industries. Our consultative methodology, rooted in design thinking, empowers organizations to drive operational excellence, enhance stakeholder experiences, and build sustainable, future-ready ecosystems.

Qyverra is committed to protecting your personal data and upholding your right to privacy. When you engage with our websites, digital platforms, or provide information through online forms or third-party channels, you entrust us with your personal information. We take this responsibility seriously and prioritize transparency, accountability, and data security in all our interactions.

This Privacy Notice outlines Qyverra’s policies and practices regarding the collection, use, storage, and protection of personal data. Our aim is to clearly explain what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have under applicable data protection frameworks.

This Privacy Notice applies to all personal data collected through Qyverra’s websites, applications, services, sales engagements, marketing initiatives, and events (collectively referred to as the “Sites”). Qyverra complies with all relevant data protection laws and regulations, including but not limited to the Information Technology Act, 2000 (India), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and other applicable global privacy standards.

Please Note: A separate privacy notice governs the collection and processing of personal information in the context of an employment relationship with Qyverra Technologies.

Personal Information You Disclose to Us

We collect various types of personal data based on how you interact with us and the purposes for which you provide your consent. This information may be submitted directly by you or obtained through authorized third parties when you visit our website, register for services, subscribe to marketing communications, or participate in events, webinars, or promotional campaigns.

The personal information we may collect includes your full name, email address, contact number, company name, job title, geographic location, service preferences, and any additional details you choose to provide through forms or communications. It is your responsibility to ensure that all information you provide is accurate, complete, and up to date, and to inform us of any changes to maintain data integrity and compliance with applicable data protection regulations.


Information Automatically Collected

Certain technical and usage-related information—such as your IP address, browser type, and device identifiers—is automatically collected when you interact with our sites. This data does not directly identify you personally (such as by name or contact details), but may include information such as your IP address, browser version, device type and hardware model, operating system, language and locale settings, referring and exit URLs, approximate geographic location, pages visited, timestamps, clickstream patterns, and other technical diagnostics.

This information is essential for ensuring the security, stability, and performance of our digital platforms. It also enables us to analyze user behavior, improve service functionality, and conduct internal reporting and optimization. Like many organizations, Qyverra also uses cookies and similar tracking technologies—including web beacons, pixels, and scripts—to enhance user experience, remember preferences, personalize content, and measure site performance.
