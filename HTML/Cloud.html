Cloud Transformation for High-Tech Enterprises

Qyverra Software’s High-Tech practice enables semiconductor OEMs, ODMs, integrated device manufacturers, fabless design firms, and independent software vendors to accelerate cloud adoption across their product lifecycle. We specialize in migrating compute-intensive applications and engineering workflows—from research and design to development and manufacturing—into private, public, and hybrid cloud environments.

By providing on-demand access to scalable compute and storage resources, our solutions empower design and fabrication teams to manage vast datasets and dynamic workloads with improved agility and efficiency. Cloud-native collaboration environments enable distributed design teams to seamlessly coordinate tasks such as exploration, synthesis, place-and-route builds, and compilation processes. Additionally, our frameworks support subscription-based models for enterprise systems including CRM, ERP, CAD/CAE, and AI platforms.

Qyverra Software leverages cloud-hosted infrastructure to facilitate next-gen innovation, such as generative design, additive manufacturing (3D printing), and big data analytics for machine learning and deep learning. Through digital twinning capabilities, we help enterprises model operations, predict system failures, eliminate process inefficiencies, and enhance manufacturing precision.

Our comprehensive managed cloud services span:

  • Infrastructure-as-a-Service (IaaS)
  • Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS)
  • Software-as-a-Service (SaaS)
  • Enterprise-as-a-Service (EaaS)
  • Database-as-a-Service (DBaaS)
  • Disaster Recovery-as-a-Service (DRaaS)

    • We incorporate advanced data encryption, secure design transfer mechanisms, and industry-specific compliance controls to mitigate latency and enhance the reliability of cloud-based silicon design workflows.

    Qyverra Software also develops custom APIs for cloud-to-cloud migration and the integration of multi-cloud ecosystems, including AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform. Our expertise with DevOps automation and open-source technologies—such as Apache Spark, Hadoop, and Hive—allows us to streamline backend services, optimize service quality, and enhance the scalability of cloud architectures.
